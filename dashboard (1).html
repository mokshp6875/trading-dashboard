    📖 How to Read This Dashboard
    Win Rate
    Of every BUY trade taken, what % closed higher the next day. Above 55% is strong. The model targets 53–57%.
    Profit Factor
    Total dollars won ÷ total dollars lost. Above 1.0 = profitable. 1.5+ is strong. This matters more than win rate alone because a 50% win rate with big wins and small losses still makes money.
    Model Confidence
    Score from 0–100% — how confident the model is the stock goes up tomorrow. Above 55% = BUY signal. Made from rule-based signals (70%) + machine learning (30%).
    Signal Strength (pips)
    How many of the model's 9 conditions passed today. Green bars = 7–9 conditions met. Yellow = 4–6. Red = 1–3. More green = higher quality signal.
    Volume
    How much stock traded compared to the 20-day average. 🔥 High volume on a BUY day = strong conviction. 🔇 Low volume moves are less reliable and more likely to reverse.
    Day Change
    How much the stock moved that day (open to close). Green = stock went up that day. Red = went down. This shows the actual price action for each day in the table.
    Stock vs SPY Chart
    Both lines start at 100. Stock above the orange SPY line = beating the market. Stock below = market is outperforming it. Numbers are not prices — they show relative performance from the start of the window.
    Market Regime (top right)
    Describes the current market environment. Bull Trend = healthy uptrend, best conditions for BUY signals. Choppy/Sideways = mixed signals, be cautious. Bear Trend = downtrend, model will sit out most days.
    10-Day Outlook
    A plain-English summary of what the model sees for the next 10 trading days. Changes automatically when you switch stocks. Based on trend, momentum, volatility, and macro conditions — not a guarantee.
    Live Forward Tracker
    Tracks model accuracy from today forward — separate from the historical backtest. Set your start date, re-run main.py each day, and re-upload to see live performance build over time.
    ⚡ Missed vs ❌ Loss
    Missed = zero money lost. Model said NO TRADE but stock went up — opportunity skipped, capital protected. Loss = money actually lost. Model said BUY and stock went down.
    RSI / MACD / Bollinger / VIX
    Technical indicators the model reads. RSI = momentum speed. MACD = buyers vs sellers. Bollinger Bands = where price sits relative to recent volatility. VIX = overall market fear level. All explained in the Why panel.
